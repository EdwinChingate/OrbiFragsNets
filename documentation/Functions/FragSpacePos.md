## Description
`FragSpacePos` maps every detected MS2 peak to all chemically plausible fragment formulae by calling [`MoleculesCand`](../Functions/MoleculesCand.md). It loops through the MS2 peak list, uses the peak-specific uncertainty (standard deviation, confidence interval, number of datapoints), and collects the Cartesian product of candidate fragments across peaks. Scientifically, this forms the annotated search space that will later be constrained by chemical consistency and fragment networks.

---
## Key operations
- Iterates over each MS2 peak (`SpectrumPeaks`) and extracts the peak mass, relative intensity, confidence interval, standard deviation, and data-point count derived from [`MSPeaksIdentification`](../Functions/MSPeaksIdentification.md).
- Calls [`MoleculesCand`](../Functions/MoleculesCand.md) with the peak-specific uncertainty envelope and user-provided `MaxAtomicSubscripts` to enumerate candidate formulae per peak.
- Concatenates candidate matrices across all peaks, forming `AllPeaksAllPossibleFragments`, which lists every fragment’s composition, mass error, and metadata.

---
## Parameters
- `SpectrumPeaks (np.ndarray)`: Table of MS2 peaks containing columns for mean m/z, standard deviation, number of centroid points, confidence interval, predicted masses, and relative intensities.
- `MaxAtomicSubscripts (np.ndarray)`: Per-element upper bounds for fragment enumeration; typically derived from the precursor molecular formula guess.

---
## Input
- `SpectrumPeaks`: Generated by [`MS2Spectrum`](../Functions/MS2Spectrum.md) or [`GetMS2forFeature`](../Functions/GetMS2forFeature.md).
- `MaxAtomicSubscripts`: Usually taken from the current precursor formula candidate produced inside [`AnnotateSpec`](../Functions/AnnotateSpec.md).

---
## Output
- `AllPeaksAllPossibleFragments`: Matrix in which each row corresponds to a fragment formula candidate alongside its mass error, predicted mass, measured m/z, confidence interval, relative intensity, and statistical descriptors.

---
## Functions
- [`MoleculesCand`](../Functions/MoleculesCand.md): Generates candidate formulas per peak under m/z uncertainty constraints.

---
## Called by
- [`AnnotateSpec`](../Functions/AnnotateSpec.md): Uses the aggregated fragment space as input for chemical consistency checks and network scoring.
